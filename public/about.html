About Batcat

Batcat is not the hero the city asked for, but he showed up anyway because someone opened a can of tuna. By day, he sleeps in suspiciously dramatic positions. By night, he patrols the living room, fighting dangerous enemies such as plastic bags, shadows, and the red laser dot.

Unlike other superheroes, Batcat does not wear a cape because it gets in the way of grooming. His greatest powers include disappearing when it is time for a bath, hearing a snack packet open from three rooms away, and knocking objects off tables while maintaining direct eye contact.

Batcat believes in justice, bravery, and receiving food exactly five minutes earlier than scheduled. He may look mysterious and intimidating, but deep inside, he is just a fluffy little criminal with excellent branding.
